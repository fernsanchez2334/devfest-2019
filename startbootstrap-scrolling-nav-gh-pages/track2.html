Sustainability Junior

Go Strawless

Your Starbucks does not need a straw all the time! Invest in a reusable straw to save you money and feel good about the environment. The turtles will appreciate it!

Support Farmers Markets

Being aware of where your products come from is a great way to know how your economic choices affect the environment. Shopping from farmers markets instead of major supermarkets will give you healthier options and allows you to support crops grown naturally without harmful pesticides.

Recycle Your Clothes

Got some old clothes you don’t use anymore? Don’t throw them away just yet! Donate your clothing to give back to others and help reuse and reduce!
