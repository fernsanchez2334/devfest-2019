Sustainability Novice

Saving Water

Reduce your water consumption in order to help preserve our planet! Simple acts such as decreasing your shower time, turning off the faucet as you brush your teeth, and fixing leaky faucets can help you with this goal.

Recycling

Recycling can have a major impact on our environment. Setting up blue and green recycling bins throughout your home, office, and school can encourage recycling for you and others.

Public Transportation

New transportation methods can help decrease your carbon footprint. Now than ever it is easier to use your bike or scooter to help you get around. Have a long commute? Take the subway or the bus and help decrease the burning of fossil fuels.

Go Paperless

Tired of having those pesky paper receipts pollute our planet? Simple changes such going paperless and switching to electronic methods can help you stay organized while saving trees and avoid pollution.
