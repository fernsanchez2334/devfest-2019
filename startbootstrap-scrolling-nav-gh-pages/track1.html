Sustainability Novice

Saving Water

Reduce your water consumption in order to help preserve our planet! Simple acts such as decreasing your shower time, turning off the faucet as you brush your teeth, and fixing leaky faucets can help you with this goal.

Recycling

Our planet is our home, and it should continue to be a home for many years to come. As the human population continues to increase at an exponential rate, our needs increase too. This ultimately leads for more of our limited resources to be used, causing climate change, the destruction of ecosystems, and deprivation of the same planet quality for future generations to come. However, changes in our actions can help decrease these concerns while still being able to meet our needs. Finding ways to help sustain ourselves while also giving importance to how our actions affect our environment will help minimize damage and emphasize practices that have a positive impacts. Change matters- and it starts with you.
